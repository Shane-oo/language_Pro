This is the most basic of the greetings, Remember the h is silent!

Buenos días

Literally meaning “good day,” it can also mean “good morning.” “Buenos días” is usually used until noon.

Perfect for those early risers, you'll find a lot of your travel tours or if you plan on hiking, will start in the morning so its ideal to say good morning with Buenos días

Buenas tardes

If you want to say “good afternoon,” and it’s one o’clock or later in the day, you can say “buenas tardes.” In Spain it may be used until later in the evening, while in most Latin American countries and the Caribbean, it may be used until the sun goes down.

Buenas noches

For saying "good evening". This phrase also means “goodnight.” But always be mindful of the context since you could be saying goodbye.
